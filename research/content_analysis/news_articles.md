## Media Coverage Summary

### Key Allegations from Media Investigations:
1. **Political Influence**: Systematic influence on AfD inner-party processes
2. **Financial Support**: Luxury hotel payments for AfD politicians (€6,192 for three politicians)
3. **Business Network**: Complex network of ~30 online platforms and investment firms
4. **International Connections**: Links to Austrian political networks
5. **Cross-Party Reach**: Contacts beyond AfD to FDP, Greens, SPD, CDU

### Rohrböck's Response:
- Denies right-wing political agenda
- Claims to be "libertarian" not "right-wing"
- Admists broad networking across political spectrum
- States conversations never led to official AfD consulting
- Former local CDU chairman in Seligenstadt

### Notable Politicians Mentioned:
- **Alice Weidel** (AfD): Multiple luxury hotel meetings since 2017
- **Corinna Miazga** (AfD Bavaria): Hotel guest of Rohrböck
- **Damian Lohr** (Young Alternative): Hotel stays
- **Thomas Mütze** (Greens Aschaffenburg): Confirmed meeting
- **Frank Franz** (NPD): Listed as company managing director

### Financial Aspects:
- Hotel costs: €6,192 for three AfD politicians
- Payment via questionable company credit cards
- Money flows from Liechtenstein, dubious PR firms globally
- Small investors seeking profitable investments
- Claims of acting for "rich clients" as "mercenary"
